Baked Ceasar Chicken

Discription

"If you think you can't cook, this baked Caesar chicken recipe will convice people otherwise," according to recipe developer Julian Hale. With a creamy sauce and buttery crouton topping, This recipe is perfect for simple weeknights at home.

It's as easy whisking the topping together, pounding chicken to an even thickness, mixing ingredients for a savory dressing, topping it with the crouton mixture and throwing it in the oven. You'll never use your old recipe again.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad croutons, crushed
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespood butter, melted
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts (1 1/2 to 2 pounds total)
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup creamy Caesar salad dressing
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • chopped fress parsley to taste for garnish
